About Me

My love for education has been the central focus of my life.


9:00 a.m. June 5, 2027 . I am standing in a room full of civil engineering students, explaining to them the importance of civil engineering, the career paths available, the roles they will be playing, and how to ensure they have a holistic education before I start my lecture on concrete mechanics. In my room, I have a customized drawing of this that serves as a constant source of reminder of what my career goals are. Since the age of 16, when I volunteered as a classroom teacher, I've known I wanted to be a teacher. Throughout my undergraduate studies at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST), Ghana and master's education at Ecole Centrale Nantes, France, I personally gathered and organized tutorials for both my classmates and juniors, and these activities were always a source of joy for me.
